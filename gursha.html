HOW it works

Select a restaurant

    
      Once you login and set your location, you'll
      be able to see all the restaurants around your
      within your delivery range. Pick a restaurant and
      see their menu.

Browse their menu

      Once you've selected the restaurant you'd 
      like to order from, take a look at their 
      menu to figure out what you'd like to eat.
      Place your choices in your basket, tell us
      how many you'd like to order,and place your order!

Place your order

      Once you have all your choices in your cart, 
      click on "Place your order" and you'll receive
       an e-mail or SMS confirmation of your order.
       Sit back and relax and we'll work to get it 
       delivered to your door in an hour or less*.
